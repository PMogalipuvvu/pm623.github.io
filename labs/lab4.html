Lab 4 : Characterization

Objective

The objective of this lab is to familiarize ourselves with the hardware available and set up a baseline. This is allow us to leveage the harware better and allow us to replicate the manual actions using autonomous behavior. To methodically document the behavior, we need to do multiple measurements and average out the readings to obtain accurate results.

Materials and Tools Used

  • 1 RC car
  • 850mAh LiPo battery
  • Remote Control

Procedure

A


The basic properties like weight, dimensions and battery time are essential to properly callibrate and automate the RC car.

B


  1. Axis rotation drift


    The drift that the RC car accrues when rotating about its own axis will be useful when we use the sensor to autonomously turn. A few extra degrees of rotation can lead to the robot encountering an obstacle or deviate from its path.
    In the embedded video, we can see that the Rc car drifts randomly when continuously rotating on its axis. The experiment was performed multiple times rotating the car both Left and Right.
